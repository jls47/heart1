There was a woman

unlike any other


who, by chance, met a man.

It was a dark and dismal night in Northgate.

A man left work and headed straight to the pub to drown in beef, beer, and self-pity.

While he ordered a drink,

he heard a voice beside him,

saying "Would you like some company?"

He wasn't sure.

He was new to the idea of even meeting people out of the blue,

but he said yes.

So they sat, and they talked,

And they passed the night away

having a good time not talking about much of anything.

Little did he know that this woman would turn his entire life around.

What did she do?

Over the course of a year,

help him regain his faith in his self,

inspire him to be a better man,

and help him understand what love really feels like and what it takes.

Without her, the man does not feel complete.

They can now be found at the Monkey when the time is right, singing to each other in the keys of
Dean Martin, George Michael, Boss Scaggs, The Cure, Snow Patrol, and more.


But on most nights, you can find them at home.

Enjoying some movies,
maybe a game or two,
or just relaxing in each other's company.
